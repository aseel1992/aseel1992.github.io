Graduation

In August of 2014, I will be graduating from the University of Florida with a Bachelor of Science in Telecommunication. I am looking forward to what the future beyond college holds. Throughout my time at UF, I have learned one extremely important and valuable lesson: to never give up. No matter how many obstacles get thrown in our direction, we must rise above them and learn from our experiences.
